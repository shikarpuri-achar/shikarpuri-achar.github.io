About Our Store

Abdul Qayoom Achar Store has been serving Shikarpur with authentic, homemade achars since 1985. We use traditional recipes passed down through generations, ensuring the same great taste that our customers have loved for decades.

All our products are made with the freshest ingredients and no artificial preservatives, maintaining the authentic flavors of Shikarpur's culinary heritage.
